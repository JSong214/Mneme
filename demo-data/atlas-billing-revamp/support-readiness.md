# Support Readiness Notes

## Migration preview

Support needs a preview that includes old plan name, new tier name, old price,
new renewal price, renewal date, and whether the customer has a legacy coupon.

Leo said the support team should not rely on engineering labels. Customer-facing
copy must say "current price" and "renewal price" instead of "legacy amount" or
"new amount."

## Expected customer questions

- Why do I see a new tier name if my price has not changed?
- Will my annual contract price change before renewal?
- What happens if my failed payment retry also fails?
- Can I keep the old plan after launch?

## Support risks

The biggest support risk is mismatched language across pricing, checkout,
invoice emails, and the billing portal. If the names differ, support volume will
increase during launch week.

Leo wrote, "Support needs one source of truth for plan names before the first
customer email is sent."
