## Options discussed

The team compared usage-based pricing, per-seat pricing, and three fixed tiers.
Usage-based pricing was attractive for heavy accounts, but it made the first
invoice harder to predict and created extra sales objections for smaller teams.

Maya said, "Usage-based pricing made monthly bills harder for customers to
forecast." Chen added that the finance model was more stable with fixed tiers.

## Decision

The team chose three fixed tiers for launch: Starter, Growth, and Scale.

The rationale was customer predictability, simpler sales conversations, and a
lower support burden during migration. Priya summarized it as: "Tiered pricing
is easier to explain on the pricing page and easier for customers to approve."
